Meet Dr. Ankita Dhelia

Founder & Lead Instructor

Dr. Ankita Dhelia is a renowned craniosacral therapy practitioner and spiritual healer dedicated to transforming lives through the art and science of touch. With years of rigorous training in biodynamic craniosacral therapy and deep study of classical osteopathic texts, she has dedicated her life to creating a bridge between ancient healing wisdom and modern therapeutic science.

Inspired by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar's vision of a stress-free, violence-free world, Dr. Ankita founded Sri Sri Well Being to train the next generation of compassionate, competent healers. Her unique approach integrates the best practices of biodynamic and biomechanical craniosacral therapy with the spiritual principles rooted in Indian tradition.

Under her guidance, students don't just learn technique—they learn to embody healing as a spiritual practice, becoming practitioners who heal not just the body, but the whole being.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for? +

This course is for anyone called to the healing arts—healthcare professionals, yoga teachers, therapists, or anyone seeking personal transformation. No prior experience necessary.

Do I need prior experience in healing or medicine? +

No. Our curriculum is designed to teach from the foundation up. Whether you're new to healing arts or experienced, you'll gain deep knowledge and hands-on skill.

What will I learn and practice? +

You'll master craniosacral anatomy and physiology, hands-on assessment and treatment techniques, nervous system work, trauma-informed practice, and the spiritual principles underlying healing.

How long is the program and what's the schedule? +

15 months consisting of 8 seminars of 5 days each (starting August 2026). This includes 6 in-person seminars and 2 online seminars.

Will I be certified? +

Yes. Upon completion, you receive a certificate in Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy. You'll be part of our global network with internship and placement opportunities at Sri Sri Wellbeing centers.

What makes Sri Sri Well Being unique? +

We're the only school blending classical osteopathy and craniosacral therapy with Indian spiritual tradition, inspired by Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar. We create compassionate healers, not just skilled technicians.
